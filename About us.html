About Us





Amaya Lake Resort in Dambulla is a serene retreat nestled along the tranquil shores of Kandalama Lake, offering guests an immersive experience in the heart of Sri Lanka's Cultural Triangle and an escape into nature. Set amidst 50 acres of lush tropical parkland, the resort provides a perfect blend of traditional Sri Lankan charm and modern comforts. Its strategic location makes it an ideal base for exploring nearby UNESCO World Heritage Sites such as the Dambulla Cave Temple and the Sigiriya Rock Fortress.


The resort features 114 elegantly designed guestrooms, including villas and suites that harmonize with the surrounding natural beauty. Guests can indulge in a variety of amenities, including an outdoor pool, an Ayurvedic spa, and a yoga center. Dining options at Amaya Lake include the poolside bar offering a selection of cocktails and fruit juices, and 'Samara' restaurant, which serves an eclectic mix of international cuisine infused with Sri Lankan flavors.

Whether you're seeking a peaceful retreat, an adventure through cultural landmarks, or a romantic getaway, Amaya Lake Resort offers an unforgettable experience with our luxurious rooms, Ayurvedic wellness treatments, and authentic local experiences, amidst the timeless beauty of Sri Lanka.
